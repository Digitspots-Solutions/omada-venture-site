About Us

Omada Venture is a leading manufacturing company based in Jos South, Plateau State. Located at By Health Center, Bukuru Express, we have established ourselves as a reliable partner for businesses seeking quality manufacturing solutions. Our facility combines modern production techniques with skilled craftsmanship to deliver products that meet the highest industry standards. We are committed to supporting Nigeria's industrial growth through excellence in manufacturing, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction. Our team brings years of experience in production management, quality control, and logistics to ensure every project meets your exact specifications.
